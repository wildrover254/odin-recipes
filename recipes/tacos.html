Tacos

Description

Tasty flour tacos. Serve warm.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1/2tsp Salt
  • 3/4 Cups Water
  • 3tbs Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a bowl, stirring in water and oil.
  2. Turn out onto floured surface and knead until smooth.
  3. Let rest for ten minutes.
  4. Divide into eight portions and roll out into 7 inch circles.
  5. In greased skillet over medium heat, cook until lightly browned.
  6. Serve warm with your favorite fillings.
