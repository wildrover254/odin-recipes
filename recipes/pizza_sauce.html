Pizza Sauce

Description

Delicious home-made pizza sauce. A little sweet a little spicy.

Ingredients

  • 1 6oz Can of Tomato Paste
  • 6oz Warm Water
  • 3tbs Parmesan Cheese
  • 2tbs Honey
  • 1tsp Minced garlic
  • 3/4tsp Onion Powder
  • 1/4tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1/4tsp Dried Marjoram
  • 1/4tsp Dried Basil
  • 1/4tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper to taste
  • Red Pepper Flakes to taste
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in mixing bowl
  2. Let sit for a least half an hour
  3. Add to your favorite dough and toppings
  4. Leftovers can be frozen or refrigerated
