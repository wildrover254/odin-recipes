Pizza Dough

Description

A simple pizza dough that makes a chewy crust.

Igredients

  • 2 1/4tsp Active Dry Yeast
  • 1/2tsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 1/2 Cups Warm Water
  • 1tsp Salt
  • 2tbs Olive Oil
  • 3 1/3 Cups All-Purpose Flour

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast and brown sugar in the water in a large bowl. Let sit for 10 minutes
  2. Stir in the salt and oil. Mix in 2 1/2 cups of the flour.
  3. Turn dough out onto a floured surface and knead in the rest of the flour until dough is no longer sticky.
  4. Let dough rise in a well oiled bowl for 1 hour.
  5. Punch down dough and form a tight ball. Allow dough to relax for 1 minute before rolling out and topping.
  6. Bake pizza on a stone at 425 degrees F for 15 to 20 minutes.
